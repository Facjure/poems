---
author: James Whitcomb Riley 
title: A Session With Uncle Sidney - IV - And Makes Nursery Rhymes - 1 The Diners In The Kitchen
---

 Our dog Fred
 Et the bread.



 Our dog Dash
 Et the hash.



 Our dog Pete
 Et the meat.



 Our dog Davy
 Et the gravy.



 Our dog Toffy
 Et the coffee.



 Our dog Jake
 Et the cake.



 Our dog Trip
 Et the dip.

 And - the worst,
 From the first, - 



 Our dog Fido
 Et the pie-dough.
