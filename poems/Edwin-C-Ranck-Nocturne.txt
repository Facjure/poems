---
author: Edwin C. Ranck 
title: Nocturne.
---

 A cat duet.
A silhouette.
 A high brick wall,
An awful squall.
 A moonlit night,
A mortal fight.
 A man in bed,
Sticks out his head.
 Gee Whiz!
The man has riz.
 His arm draws back
A big bootjack--
 A loud swish,
Squish!
 "What's that?"
A dead cat.
