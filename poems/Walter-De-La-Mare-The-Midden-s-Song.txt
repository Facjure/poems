---
author: Walter De La Mare 
title: The Midden's Song
---

 "Bubble, Bubble,
Swim to see
 Oh, how beautiful
I be.

 "Fishes, Fishes,
Finned and fine,
 What's your gold
Compared with mine?

 "Why, then, has
Wise Tishnar made
 One so lovely,
Yet so sad?

 "Lone am I,
And can but make
 A little song,
For singing's sake."
