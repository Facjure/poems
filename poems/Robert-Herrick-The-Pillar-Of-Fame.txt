---
author: Robert Herrick 
title: The Pillar Of Fame.
---

Fame's pillar here, at last, we set,
Outduringmarble,brass,orjet.
Charm'd and enchantedso
Asto withstandtheblow
 Of o v e r t h r o w;
 Nor shalltheseas,
 Oro u t r a g e s
 Ofstormso'erbear
 Whatweuprear.
 Tho'kingdomsfall,
Thispillarnevershall
Declineorwasteatall;
Butstandforeverbyhisown
Firmandwell-fix'dfoundation.
