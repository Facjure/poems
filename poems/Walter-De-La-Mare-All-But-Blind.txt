---
author: Walter De La Mare 
title: All But Blind
---

 All but blind
In his chambered hole
 Gropes for worms
The four-clawed Mole.

 All but blind
In the evening sky
 The hooded Bat
Twirls softly by.

 All but blind
In the burning day
 The Barn-Owl blunders
On her way.

 And blind as are
These three to me,
 So, blind to Some-one
I must be.
