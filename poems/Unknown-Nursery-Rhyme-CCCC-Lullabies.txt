title: Nursery Rhyme. CCCC. Lullabies.
---

[From Yorkshire and Essex. A nursery-cry. - It is also sometimes sung in the streets by boys who have small figures of wool, wood, or gypsum, &amp; c. of lambs to sell.]

Young Lambs to sell!
Young Lambs to sell!
If I'd as much money as I can tell,
I never would cry - Young Lambs to sell!
