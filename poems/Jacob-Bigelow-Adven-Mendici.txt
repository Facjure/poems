	 Hark, hark, the dogs do bark,
	 The beggars have come to town;
	 Some in rags and some in jags,
	 And some in velvet gowns.

	En! cum canum latratu,
	Et multo ululatu;
	Veniunt mendici repentes,
	Egeni, pannosi,
	Squalentes, exosi,
	Vel sericas togas gerentes.
